Rectal bleeding refers to bleeding in the rectum. There are many causes of rectal hemorrhage, including inflamed hemorrhoids , rectal varices, proctitis , stercoral ulcers and infections.  Diagnosis is usually made by proctoscopy, which is an endoscopic test.
